Hi, my name is Jaap van Hamond. I'm a composer, producer and artist from the Netherlands, born in 1997. In 2016 I started studying Music Technology at the HKU University of the Arts in Utrecht, where I follow the composition and sound art courses.

My main focus at the moment is on producing Ambient music and performing live with this, and composing pieces for various instruments and live electronics. In the latter field I have written for string quartet and harp.
