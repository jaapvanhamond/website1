Jaap van Hamond is a composer, producer and artist from the Netherlands, born in 1997. In 2016 he started studying Music Technology at the HKU University of the Arts in Utrecht, where he currently follows the composition and sound art courses.

His main focus at the moment is on producing Ambient music and performing this live, and composing pieces for various instruments and live electronics.
